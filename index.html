IIT Mandi’s vision is to be a leader in science and technology education, knowledge creation and innovation, in an India marching towards a just, inclusive and sustainable society. In pursuit of this vision, a new Technology Business Incubator, IIT Mandi Catalyst, is being setup on the campus. In order to launch IIT Mandi Catalyst, the Institute is organizing the 3rd CII - IIT Mandi Industry-Academia Conclave.

The theme of the 3rd CII - IIT Mandi Conclave is “Technology and Business Innovation at IIT Mandi”. During this 1-day event, industry and academic thought leaders will brainstorm on how to take advantage of the strengths of IIT Mandi in its pristine Himalayan location. The Chief Guest on the occasion will be Prof. Ashok Jhunjhunwala, Padmashri, Institute Chair Professor, Electrical Engineering, IIT Madras. Prof. Jhunjhunwala is renowned for indigenous product development in a globally-competitive environment, and for incubating a large number of companies in the past.



Invitation

It is our pleasure to invite your participation in "3rd CII-IIT Mandi Industry-Academia Conclave" on May 15th, 2016, Sunday. At this occasion, IIT Mandi will launch its Technology Business Incubator - IIT Mandi Catalyst, hence the theme of the conclave is - Technology and Business Innovation at IIT Mandi.

This event is being jointly organized by the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) and your participation will help us learn a great deal from your experience. As we embark upon an effort to set up an ecosystem for budding entrepreneurs at IIT Mandi and beyond, your presence will encourage both young and experienced minds.


The objective of this confluence of industry and academia is to showcase the strengths of IIT Mandi and to brainstorm the numerous possibilities for IIT Mandi Catalyst. The possibilities envisaged as outcomes of this interaction include:

  • Collaborative Research
  • Product and process innovations
  • Laboratory support / Utilization by industry
  • Continuing education for industry with academic support

The tools that could be considered for shaping up the aforementioned outcomes could be involvement of industry in curriculum design, assigning of practical projects by industry, collaborative research and development utilizing IIT facilities, sharing of best practices and expertise, etc.

We believe that your participation will definitely lead to mutually beneficial collaborations and we sincerely hope to have you grace the event with your kind presence.

Schedule

The event will be held between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM on 15th of May, 2016.
